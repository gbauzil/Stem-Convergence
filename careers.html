Do You Know What You Want To Study In College?


Deciding to pursue a STEM career is a big step and with this there are many options as to which career you would like to pursue.


Another important thing to remeber is that with each STEM career, there are many different directions, or concentrations, within each major.


Below, I have compiled a list of some of the top STEM jobs. These jobs are not only superior in salary, but are also rewarding


and play big roles in other companies.




Mechanical Engineering


Mechanical engineers work with and design mechanical and thermal devices to find solutions to problems using analytics computer design.


Mechanical engineers are also responsible for designing, testing, and analyzing their own products like power producing machines and oversee


the manufacturing of that product.This field also has different concentrations when it comes what machines that need to bedeveloped.


For example: The performance of cars and their aerodynamics or suspension, heating and cooling, or handling robotics.[1]


Degrees offered: Bachelor, Masters, Doctoral


Average Starting Salary: $87,370 [1]
